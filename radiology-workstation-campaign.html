Product Brochure . Iconography . Banner . Eamil . Video

Overview

The radiology product line campaign project includes print material - product brochure, cut sheet and digital design - website banners, social media posts and animated video.

My Role

Leading the 3D rendering team to generate radiology/medical industry lifestyle images and managing to create banners, social media graphics and magazine ads. Responsible to design the product cut sheets for the sale department client business meeting. Manageing to design the tradeshow marketing matireal - radiology brochure and animated video.
