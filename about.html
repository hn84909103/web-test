Hi there!

I'm Allison Yang (Shin-Yi Yang), a Visual Designer who is based in NYC.
I have diverse working experience as a Graphic Designer, Animator, 3D Designer for 6+ years.

💬 About Me

I am currently an in-house graphic designer at AFC Industries, responsible for the visual design of print pieces and digital content. I manage and lead the 3D design team to create a 3D environment and product renderings for product videos and videos for showcases.


In 2021, I graduated from Brooklyn College Bachelor of Multimedia Computing. In college, I was motivated and enthusiastic about the Human-Computer Interaction class. The class introduced me to user interface and user experience design. I found I could implement the iterative design process in my day-to-day graphic design projects and I had better ideas with the target audience and led the workflow efficiently. 


I have always been passionate about using my design skills to make the world a better place and I believe a good design (in any form) will do!
