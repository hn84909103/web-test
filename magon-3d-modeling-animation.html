3D Modeling . Texturing . 3D Animation

MagOn 3D Products + Animation

Product Brochure . Iconography . Banner . Eamil . Video

Overview

This project was to generate 3D product images and animation video for the MagOn product series, the magnetic car mounts for smart phones. The images were used for e-commerce product shopping page. The animation video was for social media platform to demostrate the MagOn series' funcationalty.

My Role

As a 3D designer, I was in charge of creating the product models in 3D software and texturing, lighting and posing the prodcut as the marketing team required. The main focus were presenting the rendering product images as realistic as possible and also visually appealing to the comsumers.
